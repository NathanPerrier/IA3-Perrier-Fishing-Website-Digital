The Ambrose
Treacy College Difference

Ambrose Treacy College is structured into to three separate learning and pastoral environments. This structure comprises three schools - Junior School Years 4 to 6, Middle School Years 7 to 9, and Senior School Years 10 to 12.

Junior School

Opportunities are explored in the classroom through inquiry and investigation and beyond through participation in an array of co-curricular activities

Middle School

Through innovative programs and extensive opportunities we empower young men with the skills, confidence and knowledge to make a positive difference.

Senior School

The environment inspires young men to build their capacity to make decisions on their career pathways and ensure they are maximising opportunities.

Learning Life

All students are empowered to engage in learning, belong in our community and experience academic success. Teachers work to equip students for the responsibilities and challenges of the 21st century, aspiring to develop empathetic, worldly and highly literate students who know how to learn, problem solve, create, critique and reflect.
