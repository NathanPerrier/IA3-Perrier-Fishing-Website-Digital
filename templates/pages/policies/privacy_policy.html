Frequently asked questions

Why Can't I Upload a Post?

To upload a post you must be apart of the Ambrose Treacy College fishing club. If you are then you should Contact Us.

How Do I Join the Fishing Club?

To Join the fishing club you can Join Here. Keep in mind all requests have to be accepted by the admin or club leader.

What is the Ambrose Treacy College Fishing Club?

Our fisshing club is a school-run club that invites all students, either experienced or non-experienced to fish the Brosbane River. Every week we challenge our members to catch the biggest fish, to later 'brag' about it on our dedicated 'brag page'!

Do I Have to Pay to use the website?

TThis website is completly free! At no point in time will we ever require you to pay to use our website, it is all included in the club membership.

What does this website do?

This website is designed to further connect the members of the Ambrose Treacy College Fishing club through a unique social experience where members can 'brag' about their recent catches.

Is this website Secure?

This website is built upon the safety of its user's, priotising industry-standard features such as Oauth, where your personal infomation is never disclosed.
